Protein

Cooking ground beef doesn't have to be very difficult. Here is one way to start!

  1. Open the package: You start by opening your ground beef with a knife and putting the ground beef in a skillet. Put all trash in the garbage and contaminated supplies in the sink to be washed.
  2. Turn on the stove: It's best to cook on medium heat, not high heat so it cooks thoroughly.
  3. Meat Chop: Break up the ground meat with a spatula or a meat chopper.
  4. Stir until browned (well browned if it's sausage). Remove from heat.
  • Optional: Use paper towels to soak up extra grease to your liking. You can throw away those papertowels.
